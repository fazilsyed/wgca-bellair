How It Works

1. Select Services to Offer

Choose which services you would like to offer your guests, that best fits your property's offerings. Once the selection is made, the first version is available withing a couple of days.

2. Connect To Your Service Providers

We connect each selected service, to the corresponding provider software that you use today through something called APIs that is very common in most software today.

3. Apply Your Unique Branded Experience

We customize the entire app using your logos, colors, and images. We also add your course map assets, scorecard specs, and help content including contact, rules, etc.

4. Testing and Launch Preparation

We will be conducting final tests, and support the launch preparation for the golf course including generating QR code signage, etc. We will also help you do a soft launch.

5. Launch

Setup QR codes in safe places around the property and start letting guests use the app. We are onsite launch day making sure all is well and to lend support and answer questions.
